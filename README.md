I am a software engineer who likes turning everyday confusion into practical tools.

My work sits across **Python**, **C/C++**, **Linux**, **automation**, **document intelligence**, **data analysis**, **security validation**, and production systems. Telecom is an important chapter of my journey, not the whole story: Nokia was my first phone and later my first dream company, where I learned what reliable software looks like under real operational pressure.

## How I Think

- Start from a real user pain point.
- Turn uncertainty into a workflow.
- Build the smallest useful system.
- Add tests, automation, documentation, and operational thinking.
- Keep the software understandable enough for other people to trust and extend.

## Featured Practical Projects

### [Auslander Doc Assistant](https://github.com/amandesi13/auslander-doc-assistant)

Privacy-first document assistant for international students and newcomers in Germany. It analyzes bureaucracy documents, extracts deadlines and required documents, creates action checklists, and drafts polite German replies.

**Why it matters:** It solves an everyday problem I personally understand.

### [Technical PDF Translator for Students](https://github.com/amandesi13/pdf-de2en-ocr-lens)

German-to-English technical PDF translator that extracts normal PDF text and OCRs diagrams, screenshots, labels, and scanned graphics.

**Why it matters:** Technical material often hides meaning inside figures, not just paragraphs.

### [OAI RAN Metrics xApp Lab](https://github.com/amandesi13/oai-ran-metrics-xapp-lab)

OpenAirInterface-inspired RAN observability lab with simulated gNB/cell/UE KPIs, xApp-style metric collection, anomaly detection, and Prometheus/Grafana monitoring.

**Why it matters:** It connects my Nokia production chapter with my upcoming ComNets/OAI research direction.

### [AI-Assisted Firewall Validation Engine](https://github.com/amandesi13/AI-Assisted-Firewall-Validation-Engine)

Firewall rule validation tool that simulates packet flow, detects shadowed and overly broad rules, and supports security-oriented pre-deployment checks.
